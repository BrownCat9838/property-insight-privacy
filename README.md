# Privacy Policy for NSW Property Insight

**Last Updated: April 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how **NSW Property Insight** ("the Extension") handles information. Our goal is to provide a transparent, data-driven research tool for the Sydney property market while respecting your privacy.

### 1. Information Collection and Use
We believe in a "privacy-first" approach.
* **No Financial Information:** We **do not** collect, store, or have access to your personal financial records, credit scores, bank details, or legal documents.
* **Search Data:** When you use the Extension to analyze a listing, the property address and budget are sent to our secure backend (hosted on Render) to calculate the Market Match Index.

### 2. Data Aggregation & Heat Maps
To help our community understand market trends, we perform **anonymized data aggregation**:
* **Aggregated Insights:** Your address searches are combined with thousands of others to generate **suburb-level heat maps** and market sentiment scores.
* **Anonymity:** This data is stripped of any individual identifiers. We look at *what* addresses are being searched, not *who* is searching them. This helps identify which Sydney suburbs are currently "heating up" in real-time.

### 3. Local Storage
The Extension uses `chrome.storage.local` to track your daily usage (5 free insights per day). This data remains on your local device and is not synced to our servers.

### 4. Third-Party Services
We use secure cloud infrastructure (Render and Supabase) to process property data. These services are used solely to provide the analytical functions of the Extension.

### 5. Contact & Support
If you have questions or want to support the development (and our pets! 🐾), feel free to contact us through the Chrome Web Store support link.
